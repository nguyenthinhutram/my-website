Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account?

Creating an account is easy! Click on the "Sign Up" button in the top right corner of the homepage. You'll need to provide your university email address, create a password, and fill in some basic information. Once you verify your email, you're ready to start buying and selling!

Is Campus Marketplace free to use?

Yes! Campus Marketplace is completely free for university students. We don't charge any listing fees, transaction fees, or membership fees. Our goal is to provide an affordable platform for students to buy and sell items within their campus community.

How do I list an item for sale?

To list an item, click on the "Sell Item" button in the navigation menu. Fill out the listing form with details about your item, including title, description, price, category, and condition. Add clear photos of your item, and then submit your listing. It will be visible to other users immediately!

How do payments work?

Campus Marketplace doesn't handle payments directly. We recommend meeting in person at a safe location on campus to exchange items and payment. Popular payment methods among our users include cash, Venmo, PayPal, and Zelle. Always make sure to meet in a public place and follow our safety guidelines.

What if I have an issue with a buyer or seller?

If you encounter any issues with another user, please contact our support team immediately. You can report users directly through their profile page or by emailing support@campusmarketplace.com. We take all reports seriously and will investigate promptly to maintain a safe and trustworthy community.
